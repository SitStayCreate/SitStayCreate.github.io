About

SitStayCreate is an imperative to make something. At the intersection of diy audio and open-source software communities, it is a place to imagine not how things are, but how they might be.

SitStayCreate was first envisioned in Austin, TX in 2018. We believe the average person has more than they need to make interesting instruments and compelling recordings at home. We hope to encourage others to explore the development of their own electronic instruments by sharing our projects.
