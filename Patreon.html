Fundamentals

This website does not host ads and is completely non-commercial. The software I write is also 100% non-commercial. This is separate from my music and any related merch sales. It is created in the spirit of the old web as well as to encourage others to do the same.

I am passing the hat and asking kindly for any donations to help cover the costs of owning a domain, paying for an SSL certificate, and also costs for my email address. While this currently comes out to about the cost of one takeout meal per month, I hope to grow this project.

I offer internships and the primary purpose of this site is educational. Technology can be fun and I hope to inspire others through my work.

100% of your donations will be invested in the educational and open-source aspects of this project, as well as to help keep the lights on.
