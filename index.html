SitStayCreate

SitStayCreate was created as a student project in 2017. It was a hypothetical business for a Udacity course on Android programming offered through Google's GWG program. In 2019, it became the name for a number of open-source projects focusing on electronic musical instruments and controllers. In 2024, after 7 years of existence, it became SitStayCreate LLC. We offer IT consulting services for electronic musicians and studios.
